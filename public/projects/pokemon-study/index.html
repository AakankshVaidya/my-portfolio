Research Study

How does data presentation influence investment decisions?

Thank you for taking the time to participate in my study. I'm a senior at Dartmouth College working on my Quantitative Social Science thesis, and I'm collecting data on how the visual design of financial dashboards affects the way people evaluate collectible products as investments. This study takes approximately 10 minutes to complete.

What you'll do

  • Answer 5 questions about your background and experience
  • Review two different dashboards showing data on Pokémon collectibles
  • Rate each one and tell me which factors influenced your judgment
  • Answer 2 final questions comparing your overall experience

Consent and privacy

This is anonymous research. I do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifying information. Your responses are stored with a randomly generated identifier that I cannot link back to you.

I capture how you interact with the dashboards — what you click, how long you spend on each section — for academic analysis. This helps me understand how visual presentation affects financial judgment.

You may stop participating at any time by closing this window. Partial data from incomplete sessions may be retained but cannot be linked to you.

This study is part of my QSS 82 thesis project at Dartmouth College.
