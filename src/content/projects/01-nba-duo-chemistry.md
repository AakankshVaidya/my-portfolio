## Overview

Used NBA two-player lineup data to create Pair Impact Score (PIS), a per-minute chemistry metric that weights plus-minus, assists, rebounds, steals, blocks, turnovers, and fouls. Features a force-directed network graph where edge color and thickness encode chemistry strength, alongside a horizontal bar chart of raw plus-minus for comparison.
